The identity carries the charter: fluid, agnostic, verified.

## Motif

- **Ocean** — the living, adaptive charter; a system that flows and drifts
  rather than freezing.
- **Circuitry** — the platform layer woven through the water; orchestration
  without losing fluidity.
- **Chrome ΩV** — Ω (the end / the whole) and v (the version, always
  provisional); the sigil of a system that continues.

## Application in the product

The verification terminal (`templates/index.html`) opens on the color badge
as a brief boot splash, then hands off to the deliberately monochrome
interface. The brand appears at the threshold; the work itself stays
high-contrast and friction-first — you see the identity, then you see the
cost.
